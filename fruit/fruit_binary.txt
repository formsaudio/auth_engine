Fruits are organized from smallest for smallest numbers to biggest for biggest numbers.
Users should never have to convert, but this makes some operations easy to comprehend (especially ordering)

Cherry - 2
Lime - 4
Pear - 8
Apple -16
Peach - 32
Orange - 64
Banana - 128
Mango - 256
Grapefruit - 512
Pineapple - 1024
Mellon - 2048
Watermellon - 5096
